Interactive, step-by-step prompts to help agents through calls and chats, ensuring consistency and compliance.

Features

  • ★ Dynamic Prompts that adapt to inputs
  • ★ Structured scripts with branching logic
  • ▲ Suggested phrasings and tone guidance
  • ▲ Integration-ready export (CSV)

Step 1: Greet customer & verify
Step 2: Ask permission to review account
Step 3: Suggest resolution paths
Step 4: Close with confirmation

About ARBIS

ARBIS is an integrated suite of agent-facing tools — guided flows, knowledge portal, complaints and VC assistants, verbatim analytics and usage insights — designed to improve consistency, compliance and agent performance.

Core Features & Intro

  • ★ Agent-facing intelligence and coaching
  • ★ Integrated knowledge and analytics
  • ▲ Extensible — designed for future integrations
